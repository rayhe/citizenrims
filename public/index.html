CitizenRIMS Crime Feed

Public crime data from Bay Area police agencies, updated every 5 minutes.

Endpoints

  • feed.json — All incidents + cases
  • incidents.json — Incidents only
  • cases.json — Cases only

Agencies

  • Menlo Park Police Department
  • Atherton Police Department
  • San Mateo County Sheriff's Office

Usage

fetch('/feed.json')
  .then(r => r.json())
  .then(d => console.log(d.meta, d.incidents, d.cases))
